about us

Our Story

Vidyalay practices flexible learning teaching method. It focuses on increasing flexibility in the time, location, teaching, and other aspects of the learning process. It is a method where students are given the liberty to choose how, when, what, and where they want to learn.

Our flexible learning method aims to enhance the learning pathway, create a better experience, and give students better access. We give full liberty to the students to choose their choice of study environment. Our classes happen from early morning to late night , depending on students' willingness. This allows the students to be more interested in what they learn and how they learn.

We are making flexible learning as the new normal!
